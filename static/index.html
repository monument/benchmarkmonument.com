Our family started creating granite memorials in 1923. Over the years, we have created literally thousands of memorials. Through the years, different materials have been used. There have been concrete, plastic and even stainless steel monuments. These materials have come and gone, because none of them do well in a cemetery where, they are exposed to all sorts of natural forces. Granite, bronze & some types of marble are the only materials that have been proven to endure. We have created memorials that have been shipped all over the US an as far away as Finland!

We are home to one of only two “Certified Memorialists” in Oklahoma. This is a designation granted by the Monument Builders of North America after serious study and testing has taken place. This is a continual process, with certification only lasting 3 years before the qualifications for certification must be re-proven, allowing for an additional 3 year certification. Our Certified Memorialist has been certified since 1988, giving him the distinction of not only being one of only 2 CM’s in Oklahoma, but of having been one the longest. This certification/re-certification process requires that we adhere to strict guidelines of conduct, as well as stay abreast of new trends in the design, production and installation of cemetery memorials.

We are owned and operated by a family in their 3rd and 4th generations of monument building. Even though there are many monument companies that have been “in business” for 60, 70, 80 or more years, rarely are they currently owned by people that have the generations of experience we do. We are knowledgeable about available materials, design, production and installation. Our experience doesn’t cost any more, yet it will give you the peace of mind that comes from knowing that we are competent in our trade.
